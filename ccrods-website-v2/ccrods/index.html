Going custom means you choose the components, colors, and character. Whether you need a rod built for a specific technique or just want a longer butt section, I can do it. The possibilities are endless.

I handcraft every rod right here in Florida. From the flats of Tampa Bay to the backwaters of the St. Johns River, I grew up fishing these waters and I build rods that perform in them. It doesn't matter if you're chasing snook in the mangroves, fighting tarpon on the flats, or working offshore for mahi — I build rods that match the mission.

I work within a wide range of budgets, and price is determined by the blank and components you choose.
